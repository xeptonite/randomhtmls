Future Goals

We have several ambitious goals for the future of OpenAI, including:

  • Advanced Natural Language Understanding: Enable machines to better understand the subtleties of human language, including idioms, humor, and emotions.
  • Real-Time Communication: Develop capabilities that allow the AI to respond in real-time, enabling seamless conversations.
  • Multilingual Support: Expand the model to support multiple languages, allowing more people around the world to interact with AI in their native tongue.
  • Contextual Awareness: Enable the AI to remember context over long conversations, making it feel more personal and coherent.
